Frequently Asked Questions

How long can I rent a dumpster?

Rental periods are flexible, typically ranging from 5 days to 2 weeks, with extensions available upon request.

What items are prohibited in your dumpsters?

Hazardous chemicals, tires, batteries, and flammable liquids are not allowed. Our team can advise on safe disposal alternatives.

How soon can a dumpster be delivered?

Our Dumpster Rental offers next-day delivery in most areas, depending on availability and project size.

Can I place the dumpster on the street?

Permits may be required for street placement. We can assist with obtaining the necessary approvals.

Are your services environmentally friendly?

Yes, we prioritize recycling and proper disposal to reduce landfill waste whenever possible.
